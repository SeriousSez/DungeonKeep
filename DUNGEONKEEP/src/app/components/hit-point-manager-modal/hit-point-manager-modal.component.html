Max Hit Points

Your maximum HP is your total durability pool. In fixed mode, this builder uses max Hit Die at level 1, then average gains per level, plus your Constitution modifier each level.

Rolled Hit Points

If you prefer rolling at level-up, switch to rolled mode and enter the final HP total you rolled for this character. This keeps your sheet aligned with table-specific rulings.

Overrides And Bonuses

This modal focuses on selecting fixed vs rolled progression. If your campaign applies additional HP overrides or special bonuses, adjust those from the character sheet management tools after creation.
